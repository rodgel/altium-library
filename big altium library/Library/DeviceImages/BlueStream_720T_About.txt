The LH79520 combines a 32-bit ARM7TDMI RISC, 8KB Cache, MMU, color LCD controller, and 32KB SRAM. Also included are a number of essential peripherals such as a DMA Controller, Serial and Parallel Interfaces, Infrared Support, Counter/Timers, Real Time Clock, Watchdog Timer, Pulse Width Modulators, and an on-chip Phase Lock Loop.
