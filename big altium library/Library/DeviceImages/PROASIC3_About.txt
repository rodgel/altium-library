The ProASIC3/E families of Flash FPGAs offer a breakthrough in price, performance, density, and features for today's most demanding applications. With the ARM7 soft core, M7 ProASIC3/E devices offer the benefits of programmability and time-to-market at an ASIC-level unit cost. The ProASIC3/E families are based on nonvolatile Flash technology and support between 30 K to 3 M gates and up to 616 high performance I/Os.
